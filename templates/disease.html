🦠 Disease Management Guidelines

Early Detection

Inspect fish every day for unusual behavior or visible symptoms.

Treatment

Start treatment immediately after confirming the disease.

Monitoring

Keep checking recovery status until all fish are healthy.

Tip: Maintaining good water quality and proper feeding greatly reduces disease outbreaks.
